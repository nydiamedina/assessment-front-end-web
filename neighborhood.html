Carretera Sur

Description

Carretera Sur is a zone/neighborhood where you can enjoy a cool and pleasant climate compared to other zones in Managua. There are many restaurants, supermarkets, convenience stores, drugstores, parks, and private schools nearby. Also, there are a lot of amenities to hang out with family and friends at night.
